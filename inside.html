The loop

Five stages between a request and a result.

Every task runs the same loop. It's how a probabilistic model becomes a dependable worker: each stage is checked, and each check is logged.

01 / 05

Perceive

Assemble the context: the task, the record, the relevant policy, the history. Work from what's true, not what's assumed.

02 / 05

Plan

Decompose into steps, select the tools each needs, and check the plan against the mandate before touching anything.

03 / 05

Act

Execute through scoped, permissioned tools, refund, update, reply, route. High-stakes actions wait for approval; everything is reversible.

04 / 05

Verify

The outcome is checked twice: by the system against policy, and by our human QA, who sample completed work continuously and score it.

05 / 05

Learn

Every correction, escalation and QA note updates the playbook. Month six is measurably better than launch day, and we can show you the curve.

Control

Autonomy your risk team can sign.

Control bolted on after the demo is theater. Here, the controls are the architecture, what a system may do is defined before it does anything.

Scoped permissions

The minimum access the task requires. Explicit, per-tool, revocable in one click.

Approval gates

You define the thresholds, refund size, account changes, anything, above which an action waits for a human yes.

Full audit trail

Every perception, plan, action and check is logged and exportable. If it happened, you can read exactly why.

Reversibility

Actions are designed to be undone. When something's wrong, rollback is a procedure, not an incident.

Data isolation

Your data serves your operation, never anyone else's. Single-tenant processing, encrypted in transit and at rest.

Humans, structurally

Our experts review, sample, escalate and own the outcome with you, in the loop by design, not by exception.

Compliance

Audited, certified, used to scrutiny.

Hugo has operated under enterprise security review for years, for platforms whose names you'd recognize. ISO 27001 · SOC 2 · HIPAA-ready configurations. Documentation and DPA available during evaluation.
