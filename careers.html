The Hugo way

It's not common sense. It's our culture.

There's a way the people here operate, it came from our founders, and it travels with every person we put on a problem. New joiners feel it within a week. Here it is, spelled out.

Push

We work the problem until it gives. "Hard" is a starting point, not a verdict, most of what we're proudest of was impossible right up until it wasn't.

Options, always

Never bring one answer. Bring two or three, with the criteria to choose between them. A recommendation without alternatives is just an opinion wearing a suit.

Ask

You can never be too annoying when you're demanding clarity. Message the founder at midnight if it unblocks the work. Silence is the only wrong question.

Ship in two days

If the plan says a month, show something in forty-eight hours, hypothesis-driven, gaps and all. A rough draft moves a conversation further than a perfect plan that doesn't exist yet.

Honestly

This place is not for everyone.

That's not a humble-brag. It's a filter, and it works in both directions.

You'll thrive here if…

  • You'd rather be trusted early than supervised comfortably.
  • Ambiguity reads to you as room to move, not a missing brief.
  • You take real pride in work most people never see.
  • Feedback lands with you as fuel, even when it's blunt.

You won't, if…

  • You need the full spec before the first step.
  • "That's not my job" is a sentence you reach for.
  • You'd rather look finished than be honest about the gaps.
  • Pace, for you, is something other people set.

How hiring works

Two weeks, not two months.

01 / 04

A real conversation

Thirty minutes with someone who does the work, about what you've built, broken and learned. No riddles.

02 / 04

A work sample

A small, real problem from our world, done on your time. We're looking at how you think, not whether you memorized the trick.

03 / 04

The deep dive

You walk the team through your sample and defend your choices, options, criteria, trade-offs. This is the Hugo way, live.

04 / 04

A decision, fast

Days, not weeks. If it's yes, you'll know why. If it's no, you'll also know why, we owe you that.
