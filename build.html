How quality holds

Accuracy is a process, not a promise.

The loop that keeps 99% honest at month twelve, because a number that only holds in the demo is not a number.

01 / 05

Spec

Our specialists turn your task into an unambiguous rubric. The difference between 90% and 99% starts here, before a single item is touched.

02 / 05

Label

Trained teams work to the rubric at production volume, and escalate anything ambiguous instead of guessing.

03 / 05

Sample

A fixed share of every batch is independently re-judged by senior reviewers. Ten percent, continuously, forever.

04 / 05

Adjudicate

Disagreements and edge cases are decided by experts, not averaged away. Hard calls get names attached.

05 / 05

Recalibrate

Every adjudication updates the rubric for the next batch. The pipeline you have in month six is measurably sharper than the one you started with.
